Southern Cabbage Recipe

Cabbage isn't the go to Vegetable but this recipe will have you coming back for more!

Ingredients

  • 4 strips of thick-sliced bacon, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/8 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 6 cups coarsely chopped cabbage (half a large head)
  • 1 tablespoon water

Instructions

  1. The first step is to cook the bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp.
  2. Use a slotted spoon to transfer each strip to a paper-towel-lined plate. Reserve 2 tablespoons of the drippings in the skillet.
  3. Heat the reserved grease over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Then stir in the sugar, salt, pepper flakes and pepper.
  4. Add the cabbage and water and cook for 5-6 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally.
  5. Transfer the cooked cabbage to a serving dish and sprinkle with bacon.
  6. Enjoy the dish!
