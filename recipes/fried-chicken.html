Fried Chicken Recipe

This will be the best fried chicken that has graced your taste buds I hope you all enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 6-8 pieces of chicken
  • 48 oz. of preferred oil
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoon of paprika
  • ¾ teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon of poultry seasoning or all-purpose seasoning
  • 1 ½ teaspoon of salt
  • ¾ teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup of buttermilk
